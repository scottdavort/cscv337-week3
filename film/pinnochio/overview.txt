SYNOPSIS: Guillermo Del Toro's adaptation of the classic Pinocchio tale takes a dark and whimsical turn, inviting viewers into a richly imagined world. The movie combines stunning stop-motion animation with a poignant narrative that explores themes of humanity, belonging, and disobedience. Del Toro's rendition of "Pinocchio" is a masterclass in storytelling, blending the fantastical with the macabre to create a film that is both a visual feast and a deeply moving narrative experience. This version of the beloved story is steeped in the historical backdrop of wartime, infusing the tale with a relevance and gravitas that speaks to the enduring nature of hope and resilience in the face of tyranny. The thematic exploration of disobedience as a virtue rather than a vice sets this adaptation apart, offering a nuanced take on the journey of its titular character.
